Akshaya Priority Scheme
-Candere.com

Candere, an Indian eCommerce portal acquired by Kalyan Jewellers sells Gold, Diamond, Gemstone, Platinum jewellery online.

Background:

  • Akshaya Priority Scheme is an offline scheme of Kalyan Jewellers, which encourages customers to plan their purchases in advance for festivals and occasions.

  • Kalyan Jewellers Akshaya Priority Scheme has around more than 35 Lakhs+ customers enrolled.

  • Akshaya Priority Scheme is a secure and convenient way to plan the future jewellery purchases of your choice.

  • Enroll and get more than what you pay for because you will
     be entitled to add a special bonus upon maturity (eg: 100% bonus of 1st Instalments.

Problem Statement / Goal:

  • To create a seamless User Experience journey for offline (Kalyan users) and online (candere users).

  • The entire project was divided into 2 parts: Enrollment + collection and redemption.

  • There are 2 scheme tenure (6 months and 11 months) plus the bonus differs on buying jewellery type i.e. Gold or Diamond

  • The nomenclature of a few categories on the website could be different from the customer's point of view.
