Gold Ownership Certificate - Kalyan Jewellers

Kalyan Jewellers is a popular offline precious jewellery brand that sells certified jewellery across 130+ stores in India and the Middle East.

Background:

In March 2020, Covid-19 Pandemic had unexpectedly hit the world, this led to a temporary closure of all the 130+ Kalyan Jewellers Stores in India. Kalyan Jewellers came up with the idea of creating a "book now redeem later" solution, providing customers with a great digital experience and a sense of ownership of buying gold on an Auspicious occasion like Akshaya Tritiya.

UX Design Process

Considering the Pandemic, we were unable to do any user research but tried to follow the five-stage Design Thinking model proposed by Hasso-Plattner Institute of Design at Stanford (d.school).

Problem Statement / Goal:

  • To create world-class UX for the new form of owning gold during pandemics where users can fill in minimum details and own gold.

  • To design an interface/ experience for offline jewellery buyers.

  • To showcase live gold rates for users as the Gold rate varies in different states of India.

  • To showcase a sample certificate for users intending to purchase gold.

  • To have an option of buying jewellery online in case the store is not available in the desired location.
